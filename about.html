How to choose harmless and really effective cosmetics for home care?

This is a common myth. The skin can stretch for only one reason - if the volume of muscle or fatty tissues located under the skin increases, as, for example, in the case of weight gain. In this case, the stretching tension occurs all the time, day and night, the increased body volume does not allow the skin to shrink back. Also, over the years, the elasticity of the skin decreases, the synthesis of elastin and collagen in the skin decreases after 35 years. Connective fibers become more rigid and coarse, skin elasticity is lost, wrinkles appear. But even in this case, the main factor of sagging is still not a decrease in elasticity, but a rapid decrease in the volume of muscle and adipose tissue, as well as subcutaneous fat, which is due to the aging of the body. Massages increase blood supply to tissues, stop age-related changes in elastin and collagen fibers. Due to massage, muscle tissue is strengthened, metabolic processes are normalized, wrinkles are smoothed, a contour is formed, swelling disappears
